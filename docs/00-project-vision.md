# Ocean Developers Invoice Suite

## Vision

Build a premium, modern and professional invoice management system exclusively for Ocean Developers LTD.

The application should feel like an internal business tool rather than a generic invoice generator.

The goal is to streamline customer management, invoice generation, PDF exporting and monthly business tracking while maintaining Ocean Developers' branding and professionalism.

The application should be scalable enough to support additional companies in the future if required.

---

## Primary Objectives

- Create invoices professionally
- Store customers
- Store invoices
- Generate downloadable PDFs
- Organize invoices by month and year
- Track business activity
- Build a clean dashboard

---

## Future Objectives (Do NOT build immediately)

- Email invoices
- Multiple invoice templates
- Multiple company support
- Payment integrations
- User roles
- Analytics expansion

---

## Target User

Ocean Developers LTD administrators and staff.
